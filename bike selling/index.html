Frequently Asked Questions

1. What happens if a transaction goes wrong?

If a transaction goes wrong, our support team will step in to help resolve the issue. We offer secure payment protection, mediation services, and a refund policy for qualifying cases. Both buyers and sellers can report problems through our dispute resolution system.

2. Are there any restrictions on what I can sell?

Yes, we have guidelines to ensure safe transactions. You can sell bikes, e-bikes, scooters, and related accessories. However, we don't allow stolen goods, damaged items without proper disclosure, or items that don't meet safety standards. Please review our selling policy for complete details.

3. What if the bike I buy isn't as described?

We offer buyer protection and dispute resolution to ensure fair outcomes and peace of mind.

4. How do I know if a bike is in good condition?

We recommend checking detailed photos, reading the description carefully, and asking the seller specific questions. Look for clear images of any wear or damage. Many sellers also provide maintenance records. You can always request additional photos or arrange an in-person inspection before purchase.

5. Can I delete my listing after posting?

Yes, you can delete your listing at any time through your seller dashboard. However, if you have active negotiations or pending offers, we recommend communicating with interested buyers first. Once sold, the listing will automatically be marked as completed.

6. Can I sell bike parts or accessories?

Absolutely! You can sell bike parts, accessories, helmets, lights, locks, and other cycling-related items. Just make sure to provide clear photos and accurate descriptions of the condition and compatibility of your items.
